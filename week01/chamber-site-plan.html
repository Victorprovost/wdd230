Abuja Chamber of Commerce

Site Plan

Site Name

Abuja Chamber of Commerce

Reason: This name was chosen because it clearly identifies the chamber's focus on supporting commerce in Abuja, Nigeria's capital city, a hub for business and government activities.

Site Purpose

The Abuja Chamber of Commerce website exists to:

  1. Connect local businesses with resources and networking events to boost growth in Abuja.
  2. Share information about economic opportunities and advocate for policies that benefit the business community.

Scenarios

Questions from our target audience (business owners, entrepreneurs, and investors):

  • What networking events are happening this month in Abuja for businesses?
  • Where can I find a list of chamber members and their contact details?
  • How has Abuja’s business landscape changed over the past year?

Color Schema

Selected colors reflect professionalism and growth:

  • Blue (#3498db): Used for headers, footers, and headings to symbolize trust and stability.
  • Green (#2ecc71): Applied to accents and secondary elements to represent growth and prosperity.

Typography

Chosen fonts ensure readability and professionalism:

  • Georgia: Used for headings (h1-h6) for a serif, authoritative look.
  • Arial: Used for body text for clean, sans-serif readability.
